Our Mission

We are determined to delivering compassionate, respectful, and excellent care to those we serve. We want our patients to feel tender loving care. Our health care providers listen intensely as their patients discuss their health issues and symptoms. We believe that when the patient-doctor relationship is strong your health improves. We follow up with our patients to ensure that the best measures have been taken to heal their symptoms.

Our Services

We provide a full array of health services. At our Ambulatory Surgery Center patients receive same-day surgeries and medical procedure by expert physicians from Univeristy of Maryland School of Medicine.

Our Department of Dermatology serves as a regional center for outpatient and inpatient care for patients with cutaneous diseases. Board certified Family Medicine physians give the best of care in Family Medicine ward. We have more than 40 different health services at our facility.
